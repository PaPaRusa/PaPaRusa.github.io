Help Center

How do I run a phishing test?
Ozran makes it easy to launch realistic phishing simulations. Simply log into your dashboard and go to the “Run Phishing Test” section. There, you can add employee emails individually, group them (e.g., HR, Managers), and filter them for specific campaigns. Once ready, click “Send Test” — employees will receive a real-world phishing simulation, and their responses will be tracked anonymously.
Can I group employees by department?
Yes, and we recommend it! When adding employees, choose a group such as General, HR, or Managers. Grouping allows you to send targeted simulations, track performance per department, and even deliver customized training depending on the department's risk level. It keeps your tests organized and more effective.
What happens when someone clicks a phishing link?
If an employee clicks the simulated phishing link, Ozran logs it as a failed test. We record: the timestamp of the click, their public IP address, and the browser/device used. These details appear in the “Test Results” tab. This feedback helps you identify who needs additional training — without publicly shaming anyone.
Can I export test results?
Absolutely. Go to the “Test Results” section of your dashboard and click “Export to CSV.” This file includes all tested users, their test status, date of interaction, IP, and browser info. It’s great for compliance reports, audits, or internal reviews.
How do I change my billing information?
You can update your billing details in the “Profile Settings” page under the Billing Address or Payment Method sections. Update your card or address securely — changes take effect immediately for future invoices.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel at any time. Navigate to the Billing Page, select your current plan, and choose “Cancel Subscription.” You'll retain access for the rest of your billing cycle, and we’ll never auto-charge you again.
Can I try Ozran before buying?
Yes! You can run a free phishing test from the homepage. No credit card required. This gives you a sample of how the platform works before committing to a paid plan.
Is this platform secure?
Security is at the heart of Ozran. We use end-to-end encryption, secure HTTPS protocols, industry-grade phishing engines, anonymized test data, and zero-trust architecture. Our platform is audited regularly and built with privacy in mind.
I forgot my password. What now?
No worries. On the Login Page, click “Forgot Password?” Enter your registered email, and you’ll receive a secure reset link. If it doesn’t arrive in 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How can I contact support?
You can reach us by emailing support@ozran.net. We aim to reply within 24 hours on business days. You can also visit this Help Center for quick answers, or submit a ticket via the Contact Us page if you're logged in.
